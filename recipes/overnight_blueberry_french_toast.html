Overnight Blueberry French Toast

About the recipe

This delicious and tasteful toast gives you an amazing sourly sweet experience on the palette. Having these overnight makes them easy to make as long as you are prepared.


Ingridients

French toast casserole:

  • 12 eggs, beaten
  • slices of day old bread
  • 1 cup of maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Blueberry sauce

  • 10 blueberries
  • 1 spoon of white sugar
  • 1 cup of water

Steps

  1. Step

    2. Crack the eggs and mix it with the bread


  2. Step

    3. Mix this with the maple syrup and vanilla extract. Let it sit for 20 minutes


  3. Step

    4. Mix together the ingridients of the blueberry sauce and pour it over the toast
